About This Data

Data Sources and Definitions

This governance data is collected from the va.gov-team repository where VFS teams track their Collaboration Cycle participation.

Metric Definitions

  • Touchpoints Held: Total collaboration cycle touchpoints conducted by the governance team (issues labeled with "governance-team" and touchpoint type labels that were created in the quarter)
  • Participating Teams: VFS teams with open "collaboration-cycle" issues (currently in process)
  • Products Shipped: Teams that completed Staging Review (issues with both "collaboration-cycle" and "staging-review" labels that are closed)
  • Staging Issues: Total number of issues filed during Staging Review with labels "CC-Dashboard", "Staging", and "collab-cycle-feedback"
  • Launch-Blocking Issues: Staging Review issues that also have the "launch-blocking" label
  • Launch-Blocking Percentage: The percentage of staging issues that are launch-blocking
